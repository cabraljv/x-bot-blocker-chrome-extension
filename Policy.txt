Privacy Policy for the X Bot Blocker Chrome Extension

Effective Date: 2023-12-19

Introduction
Welcome to the X Bot Blocker Chrome Extension. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our extension. Our extension is designed to identify and block bots on Twitter, enhancing your browsing experience.

Information Collection
The X Bot Blocker extension may collect the following types of information:

Usage Data: Information on how you interact with the extension, such as settings preferences and frequency of use.
Twitter Interaction Data: Information related to your interactions with Twitter, necessary for the extension to identify and block bots.
Use of Information
The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

To ensure the proper functioning and improvement of the extension.
To identify and block bots on Twitter.
To analyze trends and user behavior to enhance the extension's effectiveness.
Data Storage and Security
We are committed to protecting your information. The data collected by the extension is stored securely and is only accessed for maintenance and improvement of the extension's functionality.

Data Sharing
We do not share any personally identifiable information with third parties. Information collected by the extension is solely used for its intended purpose of improving your browsing experience on Twitter.

User Consent
By using the X Bot Blocker extension, you consent to the collection and use of information as outlined in this policy.

Changes to This Privacy Policy
We reserve the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be posted promptly on this page. Your continued use of the extension after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.
